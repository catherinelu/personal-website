About This Project

The Stanford Walking Guild is an on-campus organization committed to helping the 14,000 Stanford Staff lead happier, healthier lives.


Why Walk?

There are countless reasons to walk. Here’s a few you might not know.

Did you know that walking 30 minutes a day for 5 days a week can:

  • lower bad cholesterol
  • raise good cholesterol
  • lower blood pressure
  • reduce risk of, or manage, type 2 diabetes
  • manage your weight
  • improve your mood
