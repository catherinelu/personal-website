Hey there!

I’ve just joined the Stanford Walking Guild--it’s an on-campus community committed to leading happier, healthier lives. We are the Stanford staff that currently walk at least 30 minutes a day -- whether that’s at work, at home, etc.

Why 30 minutes? Because walking just 30 minutes a day for 5 days a week can:

  • lower bad cholesterol
  • raise good cholesterol
  • lower blood pressure
  • reduce risk of, or manage, type 2 diabetes
  • manage your weight
  • improve your mood
